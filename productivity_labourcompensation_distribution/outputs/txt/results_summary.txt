
Project structure:
1. Firm-side time series
2. Firm-side cross section
3. Worker-side time series
4. Worker-side cross section

Key methodological choices:
- No sectoral/economic-activity extension in the baseline version.
- Firm-side module includes pass-through, labour share, and unit labour cost.
- Worker-side module focuses on real PPP wages and productivity, without labour share or ULC.
- Worker-side productivity is estimated both per employee and per employed person.
- Cross-section charts use log-log specification where appropriate.
- Scatter plots use productivity on the x-axis and labour compensation / wages on the y-axis.
- Time-series scatter plots retain the fitted line and do not connect dots chronologically.
- Labour share is analysed in the firm-side framework, including a descriptive cross-section scatter.
- Country charts use a common start year across the series being compared.
- Comparative charts across countries use a common base year across countries for the same series.
